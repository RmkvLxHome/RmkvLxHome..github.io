Triangle Alcohol Recyclers

Turning Alcohol Into Good Times, Good Deeds, and Environmental Care – Responsibly!

What We Do

Alcohol may not be humanity’s greatest invention, and when misused, it can cause irreparable harm to individuals and society. However, banning it isn’t the answer—it’s a significant part of our economy and culture. That’s why we take on the responsibility of destroying alcohol the right way: responsibly, sustainably, and without harm to the environment or others.

At Triangle Alcohol Recyclers, we use the most advanced biotechnologies (our own livers!) to safely process alcohol, converting it into substances beneficial to plants and the environment. Through our efforts, we reduce the likelihood of alcohol falling into the wrong hands, mitigate the risks of misuse, and contribute to a greener, healthier world.

Based in the NC Triangle area, we combine community care, environmental stewardship, and personal sacrifice to make a positive impact—one eco-friendly session at a time.

How It Works

  1. Donate Funds: Your financial support fuels our mission to responsibly destroy alcohol while funding community initiatives and protecting the environment.
  2. Eco-Friendly Destruction: We host carefully coordinated sessions to eliminate alcohol without harming the environment, using our personal time and health to ensure it’s done right.
  3. Transforming Alcohol: Through our innovative process, alcohol is converted into substances that benefit plants and the ecosystem, creating a net-positive impact.

Why We Do It

We believe in reducing harm. By responsibly destroying alcohol, we:

  • Lower the risks of alcohol misuse by keeping it out of the wrong hands.
  • Minimize environmental damage by using sustainable and innovative destruction methods.
  • Turn a potential harm into a benefit for our community and the planet.

Meet the Team

We’re your dedicated neighbors, committed to taking responsibility for the safe destruction of alcohol. Based in the Research Triangle Park area of Wake County, North Carolina, we combine care for people, the planet, and community well-being with a passion for making a difference.
